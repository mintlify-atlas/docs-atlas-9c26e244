## Overview

Ad sequences (playlists) in PromoMaster Pro allow you to combine multiple products and announcements into a rotating display. This guide shows you how to create effective sequences that maximize customer engagement.

## Understanding the Dashboard

The Dashboard is your central hub for creating ad sequences. It consists of three main tabs:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Products" icon="tag">
    Browse and add products from your catalog
  </Card>
  <Card title="Free Announcements" icon="megaphone">
    Add custom announcement slides
  </Card>
  <Card title="My Sequence" icon="list">
    View and manage your current playlist
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Creating a Sequence

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Your Store">
    Use the **Store** dropdown in the top-right corner to choose which location will display this sequence.

    <Note>The selected store's name and address will appear on all product displays.</Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Display Duration">
    Use the **Seconds** input to set how long each item displays before rotating to the next.

    ```text
    Recommended: 5-8 seconds per item
    Minimum: 1 second
    ```

    <Tip>Shorter durations (3-5 seconds) work well for simple products, while promotional offers benefit from longer display times (8-12 seconds).</Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Items to Your Sequence">
    Navigate between the Products and Free Announcements tabs to add content:

    ### From Products Tab
    - Use the search bar to filter products by name or category
    - Click the **+ icon** on a product card to add it to your sequence
    - Click the **Play button** to launch a single product immediately

    ### From Announcements Tab
    - Browse your saved custom announcements
    - Click the **+ icon** to add to sequence
    - Click the **Play button** for instant display

    ### Quick Add All
    Click **Add All to Sequence** to instantly add your entire catalog (products + announcements) to the playlist.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Organize Your Sequence">
    Switch to the **My Sequence** tab to manage your playlist:

    <Accordion title="Reordering Items">
      Use the **up** and **down arrow** buttons that appear when hovering over an item to change its position in the sequence.
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="Removing Items">
      Click the **X button** on any item to remove it from the sequence without deleting it from your catalog.
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="Clearing the Sequence">
      Click the **Clear** button to remove all items and start fresh.
    </Accordion>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Launch Your Display">
    Once your sequence is ready, click the **Start Transmission** button in the My Sequence tab.

    The display will begin playing immediately in fullscreen mode, rotating through your items automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sequence Best Practices

### Optimal Sequence Length

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Short Sequences (3-8 items)" icon="gauge-simple">
    **Best for:**
    - Small stores with focused inventory
    - Special promotions
    - High-rotation displays
    
    **Advantage:** Customers see items repeat frequently
  </Card>
  <Card title="Long Sequences (10-30 items)" icon="list">
    **Best for:**
    - Large product catalogs
    - Full-day displays
    - Varied customer traffic
    
    **Advantage:** More variety prevents viewer fatigue
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Content Mixing Strategies

<Steps>
  <Step title="Alternate Product Types">
    Mix different categories to maintain interest:
    ```text
    Meat → Produce → Dairy → Promotion → Announcement
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Strategic Announcement Placement">
    Insert announcements every 4-6 products to:
    - Break up product displays
    - Communicate store hours or policies
    - Promote seasonal campaigns
  </Step>

  <Step title="Highlight Promotions">
    Place promotional items:
    - At the beginning for maximum visibility
    - At intervals throughout for repetition
    - Before related products (e.g., sauce promotion before meat products)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Sequences

### Sequence Indicators

The **My Sequence** tab badge shows how many items are currently in your playlist. When active, the display screen shows:
- **Progress bar** at the top indicating time until next item
- **Slide indicators** at the bottom showing current position

### Exiting a Display

Click the **red X button** in the top-right corner of the display screen to exit fullscreen and return to the dashboard.

<Warning>Exiting a display stops the sequence. It does not delete your playlist—you can restart it anytime.</Warning>

## Advanced Techniques

<Accordion title="Time-Based Sequences">
  Create different sequences for different times of day:
  - **Morning**: Breakfast items, coffee, pastries
  - **Lunch**: Quick meals, beverages, snacks
  - **Evening**: Dinner ingredients, meal solutions
  - **Weekend**: Family packs, BBQ items, party supplies
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Promotion-Focused Sequences">
  For special sales events:
  1. Start with an announcement about the sale
  2. Show 2-3 promoted products
  3. Insert another announcement
  4. Continue with remaining sale items
  5. End with a call-to-action announcement
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Category Blocking">
  Group similar items together:
  - All produce items in one block
  - All meat items in another
  - Separated by announcements
  
  This helps customers mentally organize the information.
</Accordion>

## Quick Actions

### Launch Single Item

You can bypass the sequence entirely and display a single item immediately:

1. Find the product or announcement
2. Click its **Play button**
3. The item displays fullscreen instantly

This is useful for:
- Urgent announcements
- Customer requests to see specific products
- Testing how items look on display
